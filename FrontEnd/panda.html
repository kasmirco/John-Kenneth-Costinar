Panda App

The redesigned FoodPanda app focuses on enhancing user experience with a clean, intuitive layout, faster navigation, and a smoother food ordering process. Built with usability in mind, the new design streamlines browsing, ordering, and tracking meals, making food delivery more convenient and enjoyable for users.
